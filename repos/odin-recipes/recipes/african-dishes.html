Jollof Rice


This is an African delecacy that is said to have originated from the Western part of africa; also the rice, sauce and sometimes meat broth combo is also best enjoyed fried plantain, beef, fish etc.

Here is the recipe for those of us who are interested in having an exiting feel of Jollof.


Ingredients

  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • ½ pound fresh green beans, trimmed and snapped into 1 to 2 inch pieces
  • ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 (3 pound) whole chicken, cut into 8 pieces
  • 1 cup uncooked white rice
  • 1 cup diced carrots
